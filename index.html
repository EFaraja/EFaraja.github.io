About Me

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering student passionate about combining mechanical systems, electronics, and software to create innovative and practical solutions. I enjoy turning ideas into functional projects that bridge the gap between engineering and technology.

Currently pursuing a degree in Mechatronics, I have gained experience in programming, automation, and system design through both academic and personal projects. I believe in continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and building technology that has a meaningful impact. Whether I'm writing code, designing automated systems, or exploring new technologies, I am always eager to expand my skills and take on new challenges.
